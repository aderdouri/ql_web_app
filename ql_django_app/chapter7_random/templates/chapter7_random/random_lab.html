What to Observe

Pseudo-Random (Mersenne Twister): Notice how the points can form clusters and leave large empty gaps. This is "true" randomness, but it's inefficient for simulations.

Quasi-Random (Sobol): Notice how the points are much more evenly distributed. The sequence is designed to fill the space without leaving large gaps.
