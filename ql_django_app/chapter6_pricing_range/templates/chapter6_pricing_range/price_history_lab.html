How to Read This Chart

This chart shows the "Clean Price" of the bond over time. The general upward trend is known as "pull-to-par": as a bond gets closer to its maturity date, its price naturally converges towards its face value (100).

The small periodic drops you may see correspond to **coupon payment dates**. Just after a coupon is paid, the clean price of the bond decreases.
